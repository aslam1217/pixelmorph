Frequently Asked Questions

Is pdflu really free to use?

Yes — completely free, forever. Every tool (merge, split, rotate, watermark, page numbers, image conversion, PDF to Word, Word to PDF, lock & unlock) is available at no cost with no registration required.

Are my PDF files safe with pdflu?

Most tools run 100% client-side using pdf-lib and PDF.js — nothing leaves your browser. PDF to Word and Word to PDF send files only to your own localhost Python server, not to any external service.

How do I merge multiple PDF files?

Click "Merge PDF", upload your files, drag to reorder if needed, then click "Merge Files". Your combined PDF downloads instantly — no server required.

How does PDF to Word / Word to PDF work?

These tools require a Python FastAPI backend running locally on port 8000. The frontend sends your file to localhost:8000/api/pdf-to-word or /api/word-to-pdf and downloads the result. Run it with uvicorn server:app --reload.

Is pdflu a good alternative to iLovePDF or Smallpdf?

Yes. pdflu offers the same core tools as iLovePDF, Sejda, Smallpdf, PDF24, and Adobe Acrobat Online — with one key advantage: your files never leave your browser.
