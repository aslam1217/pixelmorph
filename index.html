Is PDF Studio really free to use? ▾

Are my PDF files safe? Are they uploaded to a server? ▾

Your files are never uploaded anywhere. PDF Studio uses client-side JavaScript (pdf-lib and PDF.js) to process all files directly in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

How do I merge multiple PDF files into one? ▾

Click the "Merge PDF" tool, upload your files, drag them into the order you want, then click "Merge Files". Your combined PDF will download instantly.

How do I split or extract pages from a PDF? ▾

Use the "Split PDF" tool. Upload your PDF, click the pages you want to keep (or use the range input like "1-10, 15, 20-25"), then click "Extract Selected" to download a new PDF with only those pages.
